Green Spinach & Kale Protein Smoothie

Green Spinach-Kale Protein Smoothie recipe

 

1 Cup of Spinach

1 Cup of Kale

1 medium banana (frozen if possible – if not use ice in smoothie as well)

2 Tbsp Almond butter

3/4 cup almond milk

1 serving vanilla whey protein
